Just say it

The demo script is six utterances — in English or French, spoken, typed, or clicked. Every voice action has a keyboard and mouse fallback, so the walkthrough never blocks.

“What’s next?”
Advances one step and reads the instruction aloud.
“Which cam screw should I use?”
Spins and highlights 118331 in the model, parts rail, and transcript.
“Show me from the back.”
Moves the camera to the back-panel view.
“Where does this panel go?”
Frames the current step and highlights the panel's target position.
“Did people mess this step up before?”
Surfaces the step's common mistake with the relevant part lit up.
“Et cette vis, elle va où ?”
Same pipeline, French reply — EN/FR toggle included.
